VEIN

VEIN is a fashion system based on an unified template. All pieces are constructed with the template by different ways of construction. It allows each separate template to be renewed and exchanged. All garments are designed with consideration of inclusivity, and can be worn unisex. VEIN proposed a community that build personal connection with apparel items by customization and fluidity.
