Favorite Food

I am a person who enjoys discovering new and unique tastes and flavours. When go on a trip I always take food as one of the most memorable and accessible way to get know a city and a culture.

But in daily life my ‘favourite’ food can be really normal and even ‘plain’. I like fries, although for health I seldom have it. I also enjoy pastries and desserts, almost all kind of them as long as they are not cloyingly sweet.
